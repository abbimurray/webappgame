Welcome to Word Game 4!!

How to play...

You are provided with a word of eight letters or more which we will call "sourceword". You are to think up seven 4-or-more letter words made up from the letters contained within the sourceword, ensuring that:

  1. Each word is only made up from the letters contained within the sourceword.
  2. You haven't reused any letter more times than it appears within the sourceword.
  3. Each word exists within the dictionary (i.e., it's a "real" word).
  4. The words all have four letters or more.
  5. There are no duplicate words.
  6. None of the seven words is the source word.

Note: Case is NOT significant (i.e., This, THIS, and this are the same word).
